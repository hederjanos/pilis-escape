Discover the beauty of Pilis and make friends

Pilis Escape is more than just a hiking organizer—it's a community for those looking to explore the natural beauty of the Duna–Ipoly National Park and the Pilis region while connecting with like-minded people. Whether you're staying in Hungary for a few months or settling in for the long term, our guided hikes offer a chance to discover breathtaking trails, share memorable experiences, and build friendships along the way.

Join us and make your time in Hungary even more meaningful!
